The Setup

Today, with AI, we can make almost anything. But the hard part isn't making things anymore. The hard part is knowing what's actually true.

Marketing is still one of the few capital-intensive functions where companies scale spend based mostly on belief — on what they think might work.

Other industries built systems to discover truth before deploying capital. Oil has surveying, sampling, and shallow drilling. Finance has risk models, stress tests, and quantitative trading.

Marketing never built that layer.

That's what Consigliere is building. A system — powered by AI — that helps discover which marketing signals are actually true before brands scale spend.

Under the hood, it's a network of agents, skills, and proprietary data connected through MCP, which allows the system to plug into any AI model and operate across them.

What We Do

Consigliere is gold mining infrastructure for modern brands.

We build the systems to:

  • Extract signal from billions of available data points
  • Sample broadly and cheaply (find specks of gold)
  • Drill down with escalating investment where the data shows gold exists

The analogy:

  • Technology detects potential deposits
  • 10,000+ core samples taken simultaneously
  • High-likelihood locations identified
  • Systematic drilling where the largest deposits sit

What we're really building: A system that...

  1. Discovers which persuasion signals actually drive algorithmic growth
  2. Compounds that learning over time.

The data structure:

Persona→ Signal→ Angle→ Primitive→ Variant→ Performance

Every experiment maps back to this hierarchy. Over time, the system learns which signals drive results for which personas — and that learning compounds.

Three layers:

  • Signal Mining — Extract raw signals from customer language: reviews, ad comments, communities, trends.
  • Signal Testing — Run structured creative experiments around those signals at massive scale.
  • Signal Intelligence — Learn which signals consistently produce results. The database grows with every experiment.
Slide 4

How It Works

1. Research & Signal Extraction

AI ingests reviews, ad comments, niche communities, trends, etc., and surfaces 200+ hyper-specific insights per week for human review. The gold is buried in raw customer language:

"I'm a pregnant woman, and I instantly started investigating everything in my house."

→ Mother's Day became the second biggest moment for Caraway cookware

"I've been on Ozempic for a couple of months, and I've been really struggling with my poop."

→ Selling fiber benefits of GRUNS to GLP-1 users became a major revenue breakthrough

2. Low-Cost Sampling (Statics)

  • Rapid creative testing at massive scale
  • Find winning signals before heavy investment

3. Drilling Deep (Once Gold Is Found)

  • Sub-angle testing to sharpen the message
  • UGC video + creator partnerships matched to the signal
  • Persona-matched landing pages
  • Email/SMS capture and personalization

4. Monthly Exploration Cycles

  • New territory is explored each month based on research
  • Cross-functional execution, tight loops, run constantly

The advantage is execution density. Lots of tight loops, run constantly, coordinated across channels.

Slide 5

The MCP

Under the hood: a network of AI agents, skills, and proprietary data connected through MCP (Model Context Protocol).

What this means:

  • Model-agnostic. Works across Claude, GPT, Gemini — whatever comes next. As models improve, the system improves.
  • Composable. Agents plug together to handle research, creative strategy, performance analysis, and reporting.
  • Proprietary data layer. The signal database sits on top of MCP. It's our data, not any model provider's.
  • Co-developed with Meta AI engineering. Core platform validated. IP confirmed as Consigliere's (signed agreement).

Why this matters: The system isn't locked to one AI model. It evolves with the ecosystem. And every experiment feeds a proprietary dataset that compounds over time.

The flywheel: Services generate data → data improves MCP → MCP improves signal discovery → signal discovery drives better results.


That flywheel builds a defensible platform.
